FOUNDERS

Bhakti Kulkarni

DESIGNER

With a foundation from NID Ahmedabad, Bhakti leads the design philosophy at NOXHA. She specializes in translating complex brand values into clear, systematic visual identities. Her work spans brand systems, packaging, and editorial design, always prioritizing cultural sensitivity and intricate craftsmanship.

Raghav Kulkarni

VISUAL ARTIST

A graduate of MIT Pune, Raghav bridges the gap between technical photography and creative direction. As a Visual Artist, he focuses on atmospheric storytelling and controlled lighting to create cinematic imagery. He ensures that every frame—whether still or moving—serves the larger brand narrative.
